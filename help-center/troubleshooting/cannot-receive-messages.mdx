---
title: Cannot Receive WhatsApp Messages
description: Quick fix for message delivery issues in Hadie Danker WhatsApp API.
---

## Symptoms

- No inbound messages
- API shows delivery status "pending"

## Possible Causes

- Webhook not configured
- Phone number not verified

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check webhook status">
    ````bash
    curl -X GET \
      -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
      https://api.hadiedanker.com/v1/webhook/status
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify phone number">
    Ensure your number is verified in WhatsApp Business Manager.
  </Step>
</Steps>
